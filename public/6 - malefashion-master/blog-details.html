Men’s Fashion, Style & Latest Trends

  • By FashionBeans
  • 2023
  • 8 Comments
share

We all do our best to stay active, develop healthy eating habits, and keep gravity at bay. But… life. Whether we like it or not, it just keeps on life-ing. Which means that sometimes—we’ll call it “between primes”—you may or may not find yourself with a dad bod. You know the dad bod. That fabled physique that’s not without its muscle tone but also not without its love handles and subtly round belly. Of course, you don’t have to be a dad to have one. But you do need to have a trusty stockpile of the best fitting t-shirts for dad bods in order to properly weather this storm.

So here are 12 of them. Budget shirts. Luxury shirts. V-necks. We’ll go deep so you can get your basics caught up to that blooming torso of yours in the most comfortable and stylish way possible.

“We’re here to help you define and refine your personal style, so that you look and feel your best, whatever your budget.”

The best fitting t-shirts for dad bods have a few key characteristics you should look for when comparing options. For starters, there should be plenty of room in the midsection without being boxy. The sleeves should also have a tailored look to accentuate your arms. Buck Mason’s Slub Curved Hem Tee is my top pick for these features and more, and Uniqlo’s budget-friendly Dry Crew Neck comes in a close second.

Most of the best fitting t-shirts for dad bods share the characteristic feature of having plenty of room in the belly region. Plenty of room, but not too much room. A glaring exception, if that’s what you’re into, would be the Tony Boxy tee from The Frankie Shop. Planting its 100% cotton frame firmly in the streetwear niche, it’s oversized throughout and would pair nicely with wide-leg chinos or a stylish pair of sweatpants. The bottom left of the shirt is embossed with “TFS,” adding a nice touch to this elevated take on the dad bod tee.

Aiden Blair
